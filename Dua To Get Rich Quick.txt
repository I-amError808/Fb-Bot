The Messenger of Allah Used To 
Say This Supplication During Fajr Prayer.

TRANSLITERATION Allaahumma innee assaluka 
ilman nafia, wa rizzqan tayyeebun,
wa amalan mutaqabilan.’

TRANSLATION ‘O Allaah indeed 
I ask You for beneficial knowledge, 
and a good Halal provision, 
and actions which are accepted.’
